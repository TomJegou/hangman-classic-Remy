   BYE  |
=========

The goal of the game is to find the word
You will have 10 attempts 
If you use your 10 attempts you lose 

You can either put a letter or a full word
If you put a wrong letter, you lose 1 attempt
If you put a wrong word, you lose 2 attempts

In a case of victory, you will be able to add a word in your selected level

Good Luck !
